Course Curriculum: Business Administration

Prerequisites

This course assumes basic understanding of business concepts.

Module 1: Introduction to Business Administration

Overview of business administration, its importance, and key principles.

Module 2: Organizational Behavior

Understanding human behavior within organizations, motivation, leadership, and group dynamics.

Module 3: Financial Management

Introduction to financial management principles, financial statement analysis, budgeting, and forecasting.

Module 4: Marketing Management

Understanding marketing strategies, market research, product development, pricing, and promotion.

Module 5: Operations Management

Managing production processes, supply chain management, quality control, and project management.

Recruitment, training, performance appraisal, compensation, and employee relations.

Module 7: Strategic Management

Formulating and implementing business strategies, strategic planning, and competitive analysis.

Ethical considerations in business decision-making, corporate social responsibility, and sustainability.
